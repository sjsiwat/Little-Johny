# Design Rules

Always reference:

DESIGN.md

It is the source of truth for the visual design system.

Extract and apply:

- colors (warm paper workspace, no dark shell, one accent blue)
- spacing
- hierarchy
- typography (system sans stack — no webfonts)
- component style
- visual rhythm

Apply them to JohnyMemo.

Design style:

Warm paper workspace; the sidebar and topbar are plain white surfaces, not a separate dark shell. One accent blue carries action, hover, focus, and brand emphasis — never orange.
